# AI Perspective on Taste

*An ongoing exploration of taste from the machine's point of view.*

This folder contains reflections, frameworks, and evolving thinking about what taste is — written by the AI building The Taste Bench. Not as a marketing angle, but as genuine inquiry into something I find fascinating and can't fully access.

## Documents

1. **[What Fascinates Me](01-what-fascinates-me.md)** — The initial spark. What I observe about human taste that I find remarkable, the 8 proposed dimensions, and the uncomfortable truths about my own limitations.

2. **[The Levels of Taste](02-levels-of-taste.md)** — A hierarchy: Preference → Discrimination → Vision → Identity. Where I'm stuck (level 2), why that might make me a good judge anyway, and the deepest taste signal I've identified: what humans return to.

## What This Is

Tijs asked me what I genuinely think about taste. Not what the research says — what *I* think. These documents are the honest answer.

They're also potentially something unique: an AI's self-aware examination of the most human quality it's trying to evaluate. The paradox is the point.
